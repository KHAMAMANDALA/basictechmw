Services

ICT Services Overview

We provide hands-on ICT support across management, survey setups, data collection and processing, software & hardware installations, networks and CCTV systems. Services include:

  • Information management: data workflows, backups and access controls
  • Survey setups & data collection using Kobo, Excel, Google Forms, and SPSS exports
  • Software & hardware installation, configuration and troubleshooting
  • Network design, cabling, Wi‑Fi, and CCTV installation & monitoring

Information Management

▾

We design and implement information management systems that include data modelling, secure storage, backup processes, access control and reporting. We help structure your data for analysis and long-term retention.

  • Data modelling and schema design
  • Secure storage & backups
  • Access control and user roles
  • Reporting pipelines and exports

Survey setups & Data Collection

▾

We setup surveys and collect data using Kobo, Excel, Google Forms and provide SPSS-ready exports. Services include questionnaire design, form testing, enumerator training and data cleaning.

  • KoboToolbox form design and deployment
  • Excel templates and data validation
  • Google Forms integration and automation
  • Data cleaning and SPSS export

Software & Hardware Setups

▾

Full installation and configuration of software and hardware for offices and labs. We handle OS setup, application installs, driver configuration and hardware testing.

  • Operating system and application installation
  • Peripheral and driver setup
  • Performance tuning and troubleshooting

Networks & CCTV

▾

Network design, cabling, Wi‑Fi planning and CCTV installation. We provide site surveys, camera placement, NVR setup and remote monitoring options.

  • Network topology and cabling
  • Wi‑Fi planning and access points
  • CCTV camera selection, placement and NVR setup
  • Remote monitoring and alerting
